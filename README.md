# SmallBiz CRM

> Run your entire business from WhatsApp.

SmallBiz CRM is a **WhatsApp-first, no-code CRM** built specifically for micro-businesses (1–5 people). It eliminates the pain of manually syncing Excel sheets, CRM tools, and invoicing software by bringing everything into one simple, affordable platform — no enterprise complexity, no steep learning curve.

---

## 🚩 The Problem

- Small businesses waste hours syncing data across Excel, CRMs, and invoicing tools — because no affordable no-code integration exists.
- Traditional CRMs are too complex, too expensive, and built for enterprise sales teams — not a 3-person shop.

## ✅ The Solution

A unified platform where a small business owner can manage contacts, track deals, send invoices, chat via WhatsApp, and sync all their tools — from a single dashboard.

---

## ✨ Core Features

- **WhatsApp Inbox** — Reply to clients, use quick-reply templates, and auto-log every message to the contact timeline.
- **Contacts & Pipeline** — Kanban-style deal tracking with a full interaction history per contact.
- **Invoicing** — Create, send (via WhatsApp or email), and track invoices with one click.
- **Integrations Hub** — Connect Excel, Google Sheets, Zoho Books, QuickBooks, Razorpay, Stripe, Gmail, and more.
- **Dashboard** — Live metrics, activity feed, quick actions, and upcoming follow-ups at a glance.
- **Reports** — Sales funnel, revenue over time, and top contacts — simple and exportable.
